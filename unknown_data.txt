The issues of religion politics became interconnected in Bangladesh 
 This article argues that the interconnection of religion and politics in the context of Bangladesh is linked with the modes of governance 
 Religious minorities are subject of threats in bangladesh in several cases of political issues 
 In other words it is a problem of politics not religion 
 The people of Bengal want freedom of religion they do not want any interference in religious matters 
 Religion can not be used for political ends
